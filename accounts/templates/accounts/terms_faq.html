Simple, professional, and trust-focused policies built for authentic peer-to-peer asset sharing.

1. Introduction

Welcome to NO MIDDLEMEN, a peer-to-peer asset sharing platform connecting owners directly with borrowers, tenants, or renters. By accessing or using this platform, you agree to comply with and be bound by these terms.

2. Eligibility

Users must strictly fulfill the following conditions to maintain ecosystem accountability:

  • ✓ Be 18 years or older.
  • ✓ Provide highly accurate and truthful registration details.
  • ✓ Upload and use their own valid National ID.
  • ✓ Not impersonate another person, representative, or asset manager.
3. Platform Role

NO MIDDLEMEN operates explicitly under the following parameters:

  • • Provides a directory marketplace infrastructure only.
  • • Is not an owner, agent, manager, broker, or middleman.
  • • Does not guarantee or verify the quality, safety, or condition of listed assets.
  • • Is not responsible for private transactional agreements or disputes between users.
4. Listing Rules

Users are strictly required to adhere to these publishing frameworks:

  • • You must legally own the asset or have verified permission to list it.
  • • You must use genuine, accurate photographs of the actual item.
  • • Provide truthful descriptions and specify clear rental or sharing terms.
  • • Avoid creating duplicate listings for the same asset.
❌ Strictly Prohibited: Stolen property, illegal items, fake listings, fraudulent activities, and offensive marketplace content.
5. Payments

Ecosystem marketplace searching and browsing are completely free. Fixed listing maintenance fees must be paid before publishing an asset to fund baseline platform infrastructure and KYC processing. All rental payments are arranged directly between parties off-platform. Listing fees are non-refundable once approved.

6. User Responsibilities

Users assume complete responsibility for:

  • • Thoroughly inspecting assets physically before finalizing rentals.
  • • Verifying the legitimate ownership of the counterparty.
  • • Signing external legal sharing agreements where necessary.
  • • Ensuring the safe handling, maintenance, and timely return of rented items.
7. Suspension and Termination

NO MIDDLEMEN reserves the right to suspend or permanently deactivate accounts for:

  • • Engaging in fraudulent behavior or trickery.
  • • Spamming multiple duplicate listings.
  • • Abuse or harassment directed at other users.
  • • Providing false or un-matching verification details.
  • • Violating local constitutional laws.
8. Privacy Policy Core

Personal information is fully protected under strict system storage guidelines and is exclusively utilized for: identity verification, accurate account management, and proactive platform fraud prevention.

9. Limitation of Liability

NO MIDDLEMEN shall not be held liable for financial or structural losses resulting from transactions, property damage during rentals, theft, or personal civil disputes arising among platform users.

10. Amendments

These operational terms and conditions may be updated periodically to adapt to system evolutions. Continued usage of the platform constitutes total acceptance of any revisions.
